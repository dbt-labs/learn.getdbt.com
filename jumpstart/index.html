Jumpstart

A four week, asychronous course to get your team up to speed with dbt.

Overview

  • This course is completed in a combination of asynchronous and synchronous sessions.
  • Asynchronous Sessions: Participants watch instructional videos online, complete Google form with check for understanding questions, and complete exercises in dbt at their own pace. Instructional videos use the dbt Cloud interface for demonstrations. The amount of time needed for each asynchronous session is about 2 hours (60 minutes of videos and 60 minutes for Google form and exercises)
  • Synchronous Sessions: A Fishtown Analytics team member facilitates a review of the exercises and answers questions that participants record during their asynchronous work. Synchronous sessions will be hosted by Fishtown Analytics using Zoom for video conferencing.
  • The course is implemented with Fishtown Analyticsâ€™ Training Snowflake account as the Data Warehouse and personal or client hosted Github repositories as the host for version control. Participants will have access to the Fishtown Snowflake account for 45 days from the start of the Jumpstart course.
  • Instructional videos are hosted online using Wistia on a Fishtown Analytics owned website. These will be updated by the Fishtown Analytics team as dbt Cloud evolves but the client will have access to the videos indefinitely.
  • Criteria for Success: Participants are expected to view 100% of videos and complete 100% of the Google Forms and exercises 24 hours prior to each office hours session. This will ensure instructors can review the completed work and participants maximize their takeaways from the course.
