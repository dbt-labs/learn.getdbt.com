Jumpstart

A four week, asychronous course to get your team up to speed with dbt.

Overview

  • This course is completed in a combination of asynchronous and synchronous sessions.
  • Asynchronous Sessions (2 hours each week): Participants watch instructional videos online, complete Google form with check for understanding questions, and complete exercises in dbt at their own pace.
  • Synchronous Sessions (1 hour each week): A Fishtown Analytics team member facilitates a review of the exercises and answers questions that participants record during their asynchronous work.
  • Infrastructure: The course is implemented with Fishtown Analyticsâ€™ Training Snowflake account as the Data Warehouse and personal or client hosted Github repositories as the host for version control.
  • Criteria for Success: Participants are expected to view 100% of videos and complete 100% of the Google Forms and exercises 24 hours prior to each office hours session.

What to Expect

Before the Kick-Off Meeting

  • Join private Jumpstart slack channel in dbt Slack.
  • Accept the dbt Cloud Invitation and Login to Snowflake.
  • Create an empty repository in your personal GitHub account and send the link to your instructor.

Kick-Off Meeting

  • Introductions and Overview of the Jumpstart Program.
  • Kick-Off Slides
  • Q+A Session for Jumpstart Logistics.

Asynchronous Sessions

  • Watch Instructional Videos.
  • Complete Check for Understanding in Google Form.
  • Practice Exercises.

Synchronous Sessions

  • Instructor reviews responses to Google Form and Practice Exercises.
  • Q+A Sessions on using dbt.

Week 1

  • Start Instructional Videos: Who is an Analytics Engineer & Setting up dbt Cloud
  • PR Template: Add this pull request template to your dbt Project. (video walkthrough)
  • Continue Instructional Videos: Models.
  • Submit Pull-Request: Practice Exercises for Models and submit a PR to your instructor with this template
  • Week 1 Check for Understanding Form.
  • Note: All practice exercises and forms should be completed 24 hours before the next office hours. This will give adequate time for your instructor to prepare for the office hours.

Week 2

  • Instructional Videos: Tests, Docs, Sources, and Deployment.
  • Submit Pull-Request: Practice Exercises for Tests, Docs, and Sources and submit a PR to your instructor with this template
  • Week 2 Check for Understanding Form.
  • Note: All practice exercises and forms should be completed 24 hours before the next office hours. This will give adequate time for your instructor to prepare for the office hours.

Week 3

  • Instructional Videos: Jinja Primer, Macros, and Packages.
  • Submit Pull-Request: Practice Exercises for Jinja Primer, Macros, and Packages and submit a PR to your instructor with this template
  • Week 3 Check for Understanding Form.
  • Note: All practice exercises and forms should be completed 24 hours before the next office hours. This will give adequate time for your instructor to prepare for the office hours.

Week 4

  • Instructional Videos: Materializations and Analyses + Seeds
  • Submit Pull-Request: Practice Exercises for Materializations and Analyses + Seeds and submit a PR to your instructor with this template
  • Week 4 Check for Understanding Form.
  • Note: All practice exercises and forms should be completed 24 hours before the next office hours. This will give adequate time for your instructor to prepare for the office hours.
  • Close out the course with final questions, feedback, and certificates.
  • Complete Post-Jumpstart Survey

Post Jumpstart

  • The last meeting for Jumpstart will take place as the Week 4 Office Hour. Once this is completed, homework and pull request reviews will no longer be reviewed. All future questions should be directed to your Account Manager.
  • dbt Slack Channel The Jumpstart specific slack channel will be deprecated a week after Jumpstart ended.
  • Snowflake Your Snowflake user will be deprecated after 45 days from the start of the Jumpstart program.
  • Jumpstart videos These videos will be always available to you. Feel free to use the videos for internal training or reference.
  • dbt Cloud Training Account This account will be deprecated after 45 days from the start of the Jumpstart program.
  • Github repository Since this entity is on your personal account, this is yours. If you need any additional pull request reviews, we recommend asking your fellow classmates to review.
