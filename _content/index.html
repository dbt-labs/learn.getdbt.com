About dbt learn

dbt learn is a three day in-person training program designed to empower analysts. The only technical requirement needed is a practical knowledge of SQL, leave the rest to us! Taught by the Fishtown Analytics team, analysts will learn SQL essentials and the in's and out's of dbt through real-world applications. Analysts should walk away with a deeper understanding of how to approach, model, and optimize data through dbt.
