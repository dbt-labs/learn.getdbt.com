FAQ

Is there a free online version?

Our self-service version of Learn is currently in development and we are eager to put it into production. The dbt docs are an excellent resource in the meantime for learning dbt and analytics engineering.

Who should attend dbt Learn?

If some of the following statements are true for you, then dbt Learn will likely be a good fit:

  • I'm thinking about building models incrementally.
  • My dbt project doesn't have tests.
  • I'm interested in learning about the latest workflows and tools in analytics.
  • I am unsure how to debug errors.
  • I want the official dbt training.
  • I'm not someone who regularly adopts new software just by reading documentation.

We have found dbt Learn to be particularly powerful for the following roles:

  • Data Analysts who have been using dbt for ~3 months.
  • Self-starters in the analytics space - some working SQL knowledge
  • Data Engineers who are new to dbt
  • Data Team Managers
  • Analytics Consultants

Is it possible to see a course syllabus ahead of time?

We are constantly updating and improving on dbt Learn! We provide registered students with a detailed agenda a few weeks before each session. The agenda lists some topic areas we always cover and our lessons are available to preview.

The cost of the event is prohibitive for me, is there any flexibility on the price?

We are committed to making dbt, and knowledge of analytics engineering, accessible to as many people as possible. To that end, we have a diversity scholarship available to decrease or waive the cost of participation.
